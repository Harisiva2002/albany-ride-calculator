      📋 How Fares Are Set

      Our fares are calculated taking into consideration:

      🚗
      Car Depreciation — Calculated for both ways, as the driver typically returns empty after drop-off.
      ⛽
      Gas / Mileage — Fuel surcharge of $0.10 per mile due to rising gas prices (~$1.60 for a 10-mile trip). This is a temporary surcharge and will be removed once prices stabilize.
      🛡️
      Auto Insurance — Ongoing coverage cost factored per ride.
      💵
      Base Pay — Fair compensation for the driver's time and effort.
